# Middle Ages Artists Biographies

This collection contains biographies of notable artists from the Middle Ages period (approximately 5th to 15th century).

## Artists Included

### Italian Artists
- **Giotto di Bondone** - Pioneer of Renaissance painting, known for naturalistic style
- **Duccio di Buoninsegna** - Master of Sienese school painting
- **Cimabue** - Florentine painter and mosaicist

### Franco-Flemish Artists
- **Jean Fouquet** - French painter and illuminator
- **Jan van Eyck** - Flemish painter, master of oil painting technique

## Organization

Each artist has their own dedicated file containing:
- Full name and dates
- Nationality and region
- Artistic style and period
- Notable works
- Historical significance
- Key achievements

## Period Coverage

This collection focuses on artists from the High and Late Middle Ages (c. 1000-1500), transitioning into the Early Renaissance period.
